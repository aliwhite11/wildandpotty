The Potter

30 years of
clay & fire

Lou has been making ceramics for thirty years. From her new studio at Shelly Beach on the Kaipara Harbour, she throws and hand-builds bowls, vessels, garden pieces, homewares, and ceramic jewellery.

Everything is made by hand. No two pieces are the same.
