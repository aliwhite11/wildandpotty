The Artist

Thirty years of
earth & fire

Tucked away in the lush coastal village of Shelly Beach on the Kaipara Harbour, our studio is a sanctuary of spinning wheels, damp clay, and creative magic. For over three decades, one passionate ceramicist has been coaxing bowls, vases, sculptures and garden treasures from raw earth.

Every piece tells a story — shaped by hand, fired with care, glazed with colours drawn from the natural world. No two are ever quite the same, because the best things in life rarely are.

Bespoke Creations

Pieces made
with soul

Each creation is a bespoke conversation between artist and clay. Browse some of the styles available — or get in touch to commission something wholly unique to you.

Vases & Vessels

Thrown on the wheel or hand-built, these stand beautifully on mantels, windowsills and sideboards. Organic forms, earthy glazes, unexpected textures.

Bowls & Platters

Functional art for the everyday table — or display pieces too precious to eat from.

Garden & Outdoor

Sculptural planters, whimsical garden totems and birdbaths that weather beautifully.

Wall Art & Tiles

Hand-painted ceramic tiles and sculptural wall hangings — bold, colourful, unique.

Bespoke Commissions

Have something special in mind? A wedding gift, a memorial piece, a set of mugs for the family bach — get in touch and let's create something just for you. No idea too wild, no request too potty.
